WHAT EXACTLY IS "SMART NATION?"

 Unveiled by Prime Minister Lee Hisen Loong in 2014,
Smart Nation is Singapore's national effort to co-create a future of better living through all tech-enabled solutions. The goal of the initiative is transforming Singapore into a highly advanced, integrated technological hub striving to develop people-centric solutions to address global urban challanges.

When Did Smart Nation start? 

"24 November, 2014"

Pm Lee Announcing Smart Nation, 2014

The Begining.

Singapore's Smart Nation Initiative was officially launched
by PM Lee on 24 November, 2014.

Managing Office

The Initiative is cordinated by the Smart Nation and Digital Government Office (SNDGO) in the Prime Minister's office (PMO) and by other government agencies.

Spearheading Minister

Minister Vivian Balakrishnan was officially appointed Minister-In-Charge of the Smart Nation Initiative.

"Mission Statement"

To get Singaporeans to embrace Smart Nation and understand what it is, mainly adults and teenagers who are reliant on IT daily, educating them on what Smart Nation is, as well as what it's initiatives and benefits are. At the same time, educate them on the importance of online security in smart nation and safe practices to take to protect themselves online.
