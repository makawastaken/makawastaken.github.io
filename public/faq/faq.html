General

What is Nexus Network for?
Nexus Network is created to help users share information on Smart Nation. Here, users can gather to share their views on Smart Nation and therefore discuss their ideas for the future.
How are we connecting users to embracing S/N?
We have two distinct systems for people to communicate. We have a forum that allows long-term discussion, allowing people to view the discussion and express their views. We also have a chatroom, for people who want to have real time discussions on the fly.
What is the code of conduct when using the platform?
The Nexus Network is an area created so that people can have an easier time accessing information and to create discussions.
We will hope that you, upon loading into the Nexus Network, will abide by these rules. Anyone found breaking these rules will be reviewed on a case by case basis.
1) Do not act aggressively against any other user. This includes racial swearing, violence and sexual harrassment.
2) Do not discriminate. This includes racial/ethnic swearing, harassment.
3) Do not advertise in any chat. This is a platform solely for users to have healthy discussions about Smart Nation, not a platform to use for a business venture.

Accounts

How do I create an account?
At our login page, there will be a link that will lead you to the sign up page to create an account. It is located under our login function.
My account has been banned! What should I do?
You can approach one of our admins by sending them a mail in your profile settings. We will then send you details on why you have been banned.
Reporting users
There is a report function in the chatroom, just take a screenshot of the chat. The administrators will follow up shortly.
How do I change my password?
Your password can be changed at the settings. Go to the top left to change your password! Do take note that your passwords have to be different from your current password!
Can I change my username?
Yes you can! Do take note that you can only change your username to one that has not been already taken.
What usernames are allowed?
As long as it does not violate these two rules, you are free to use your username!
1) No Racial or Ethnic Slurs in your name.
2) No Swear words in your username.

Chats

What is the chat feature for?
Our chat feature allows people to gather in a real-time discussion of Smart Nation!
How do I join a chat?
First you must have an account and logged in. If not, proceed to our login page.
To join a chat room, you just have to click on one of the chat channels on the left bar and you're ready to go!
How do I exit a chatroom?
Press the power icon on the top of the page and you're ready to go.
Private Messaging. Can I do that?
Yes you can!
There is a bar at the bottom of the page. Click it to enable a dropdown, and the dropdown should show two inputs for one to put in. Type the username of whoever you want to message in the first box, and the message you want to send in the second box!
