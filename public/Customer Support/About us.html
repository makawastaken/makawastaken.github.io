ABOUT US

---OUR STORY---


We started off as a small entrepreneur company focusing mainly on coding websites for other companies. We are not exactly famous but jobs came in anyway and eventually we began coding websites for famous companies. It was only then when we got our current project assigned to us by the government to educate the citizen in singapore on smart nation and the benefits of it, hoping they will understand and start embracing it. No doubt it was our biggest and most stressful project, as we have to make sure users who visit our webpage will begin to accept smart nation and support the idea of singapore being a smart nation. After all the hardship the team had been through, we finally completed our project and the website you are currently on is the fruit of our result.
